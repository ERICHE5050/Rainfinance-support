About Rainfinance

Rainfinance is a sophisticated stock analysis app designed to help investors evaluate both the overall market and individual stocks. By focusing on fundamental valuation metrics, the app provides clear insights into whether securities are potentially undervalued or overvalued relative to their historical trading ranges.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Rainfinance evaluate stocks?

Rainfinance analyzes historical trading ranges to determine if a stock is currently undervalued or overvalued relative to its own history. The app presents this information in an easy-to-understand format with valuation indicators.

Why is there a limit of 15 stocks?

This limit helps ensure optimal performance and keeps the interface focused on your most important investments.

How often is the data updated?

Stock data is updated each time you open the app, ensuring you always have the most current information available.

How do I add a new stock?

Tap the "+" button in the bottom right corner of the main screen, then enter the stock symbol (e.g., AAPL for Apple).
